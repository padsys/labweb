HPC Systems and Libraries

High-Performance Computing (HPC) systems and libraries have been playing a critical role for science discovery over the last decades. Recent years, HPC technologies have been heavily used by machine learning and deep learning applications. Advances in HPC systems and libraries are enabling researchers to solve bigger and harder problems.

(Note: Logo is designed by PADSYS Lab. All rights reserved.)

PADSYS Lab members design and develop novel HPC systems and libraries for both scientific computing applications and artificial intelligence workloads. We are particularly interested in the following topics:

  • Message Passing Interface (MPI) Runtime
  • Checkpoint-Restart Schemes
  • Partitioned Global Address Space (PGAS) Runtime
  • Hybrid MPI+X Programming Models
